Audio Instruction


1. Get an arm chair and a measuring tape
2. Measure and mark a 3 meters walkway.
3. Wear regular footwear and use your
walking aid (if already using one).
4. Start the red button timer,
when you are getting up from the chair.
5. Carry the phone while walking.
6. Walk in a comfortable and safe
walking speed.
7. Turn around at the line,
walk back the chair and sit down.
8. Stop the red button
timer when your buttock touch the seat.
